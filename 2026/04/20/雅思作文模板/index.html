二、作文二

1）好词积累

facilitate crucial practical = useful = pragmatic
promote essential
accelerate pivotal(关键的)
foster irreplaceable
boost
2、连接词
并列 递进 对比 转折 总结
primarily furthermore on the other hand therefore
for one thing In addition however
for another Moreover

2）句型积累

1、改写
  • 好好
    • Some people contend that the primary advantage of … lies in … , while others argue that it is the … that reaps the most significant benefits
  • 好坏
    • Some people claim that … , while others hold the opposite view.
  • 现象的原因
    • Some people claim that this phenomenon mainly results from the… rather than the …
  • 综合
    • Nowadays, many people argue that it is beneficial to …
2、开头
  • 好好讨论
    • While … has some benefits, I believe that … is generally more effective
    • Although … undoubtedly fosters economic growth and commercial development, I firmly believe that its contribution to … is far more crucial
  • 题目：To what extent do you agree or disagree with the statement?
    • Although it has some certain drawbacks in several aspects, I firmly believe that its positive impacts far outweigh the negative ones.（通用）
    • I completely agree with this view to a large extent.
    • From my perspective, … is much more beneficial for …
  • 好坏讨论：Is this a positive or negative development? / Do you think the benefits outweigh the drawbacks?
    • Although some people claim that this trend may bring negative effects to human life, I firmly believe that the benefits it offers far outweigh the drawbacks.
    • While this approach offers certain benefits in specific scenarios, I firmly believe that its drawbacks far outweigh its advantages.
    • Although it has certain merits in specific aspects, I firmly believe that its negative impacts far outweigh the positive ones.
    • Although … has certain benefits, I firmly believe the drawbacks far outweigh the advantages.
  • 现象（原因+观点）
    • This phenomenon is driven by several key factors, and I firmly believe …
  • 现象（原因+解决方案）（背）
    • This phenomenon stems from several interrelated factors, and there are practical strategies that individuals and governments can adopt to make tangible（实际的） progress in addressing these issues.
3、中间段落总起段
  • 否认观点
    • Admittedly, … serves as a powerful engine for …
    • Admittedly, the … can have some negative impacts.
    • Admittedly, the 现象 involves certain potential risks.
    • Admittedly, … has several merits that contribute to …
    • Admittedly, … is necessary and brings certain social and personal benefits.
    • Admittedly, it is reasonable for …
  • 支持观点
    • On the other hand, I firmly believe that … plays a far more pivotal role in …
    • Despite the above disadvantages, the … can bring significant benefits to both individuals and society.
    • Despite these concerns, the … provides substantial advantages to individuals and society as a whole.
    • Despite the above benefits, … exerts(施加) more negative impacts on … , especially in terms of …（讲坏处）（记）
    • … is far more beneficial to long-term economic growth and social development
    • While the benefits of … are far more significant.
    • Despite the reasons mentioned above, I firmly believe that … , for two compelling reasons.（现象+原因）
    • However, … will bring more severe negative consequences for both individuals and global cultural diversity.
  • 现象原因
    • The 现象 are primarily driven by the …
    • While …. is the main driver, the … play a minor role in the 现象
    • The 现象 can be mainly attributed to three key reasons.（背）
    • The … can be mainly attributed to two key factors.
  • 解决方案
    • However, there are several practical methods for both individuals and governments to address these pressing problems.（背）
    • To … , practical solutions exist.
4、结尾
  • 问题：Discuss both views and give your own opinion.
    • In conclusion, while … , its role in … is irreplaceable. Therefore, I firmly believe that …
  • 问题：To what extent do you agree or disagree with the statement?
    • In conclusion, although … may bring some problems such as … its benefits to individuals and the society are more obvious, including … Therefore, I fully agree that …
  • 问题：Is this a positive or negative development?
    • In conclusion, while … may lead to … , its positive impacts on … are far more significant. Therefore, I regard the … as a generally positive development.
