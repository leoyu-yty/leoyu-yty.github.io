2）常用句

  • The proportion of … rose consistently from 7% in 2009 to 21% in 2020.（主语为数字）
  • The years between 1999 and 2009 witnessed a significant rise in China’s GDP.（主语为年份）
  • The number of visitors to the museum demonstrated an apparent upward trend, increasing from 70,000 to 120,000 from 2007 to 2015.（主语为数字+demonstrate +趋势）
  • A slight dip can be seen in the quality of fast food consumed, from 17 units person in2019 to 15 units in 2021. （主语为下降 / 上升）
  • There was a slight dip in the quality of fast food consumed, from 17 units person in 2019 to 15 units in 2021.（There be）
  • The proportion of women engineers rose consistently from 7% in 2009 to 21% in 2025, before a predicted further rising to 29% in 2030. （两段连接before）

案例二（折线）

题目：The graph below gives information about the percentage of the population in four Asian countries living in cities from 1970 to 2020, with predictions for 2030 and 2040.
1、开头
  • The line graph provides information about the percentage of the total population living in urban areas in four Asian countries (Malaysia, the Philippines, Thailand, and Indonesia) over the period from 1970 to 2020, with predictions for 2030 and 2040.
2、Overview（总体趋势+最高最低）
  • Overall, all four nations experienced a long-term upward trend in urban population proportion during the shown period.
  • Malaysia consistently led the urbanization process and was predicted to remain ahead by 2040, while Indonesia started at the lowest level but saw the most dramatic growth.
3、Group 1

  • In 1970, Malaysia and the Philippines had similar urban populations, at 30% and 32% respectively.

    Both countries witnessed a sharp increase in this proportion over the following two decades: Malaysia’s urban population rose to 41% by 1980 and then to 61% by 1990, while the Philippines reached 49% in 1990.

    After 1990, the Philippines’ urbanization rate dropped slightly, falling to 43% in 2010, before gradually rising again to a predicted 56% by 2040.

    In contrast, Malaysia’s urban population continued to climb steadily, hitting 71% in 2010 and reaching an estimated 80% by 2040.

4、Group 2

  • Thailand and Indonesia showed distinct patterns of urban growth.

    Thailand’s urban proportion began at 19% in 1970, rose gradually to 30% by 2000, and then kept stable until 2010. It was then predicted to rise steeply to around 50% by 2040.

    Indonesia, by far the lowest in 1970 at just 14%, saw a slow but consistent increase over the first 30 years, reaching 30% by 2000. After 2000, its urban population grew rapidly, surpassing(超过)Thailand’s rate by 2020 and is expected to reach approximately 64% by 2040, making it the second-most urbanized country in the forecast.

案例三（折线）

题目：The graph below shows the number of shops that closed and the number of new shops that opened in one country between 2011 and 2018.
1、开头
  • The line graph illustrates the number of shop closures and new shop openings in a single country over the period from 2011 to 2018.
2、Overview
  • Overall, both indicators experienced a general downward trend over the 7-year period.
  • While the number of new shop openings was higher at the start of the period, shop closures exceeded openings for the majority of the years shown.（大多数时间都超过）
3、核心段

  • In 2011, the number of new shop openings peaked at approximately 8,500, which was around 2,000 higher than the number of shop closures in the same year.

    The following year witnessed a sharp drop in openings, which fell to 4,000, whereas closures declined only slightly to 6,000.

    Over the next two years, openings rose steadily, reaching nearly 6,300 in 2014, a figure almost equal to the number of closures that year.（和另一个相等）

  • From 2014 to 2016, the number of closures fluctuated dramatically: it plummeted to a record low （跌到最低点）of just 600 in 2015, before rebounding sharply to 5,200 in 2016.

    After this sharp fluctuation, the numbers stabilized at around 5,000 for the remainder of the period.

  • In contrast, the number of new openings saw a gradual overall decline: it remained relatively stable at roughly 4,000 between 2015 and 2017, before falling further to 3,000 in 2018, the lowest figure for openings in the entire period.（达到最低点）

案例四（折线）

题目：The charts below show the changes in ownership of electrical appliances and amount of time spent doing housework in households in one country between 1920 and 2019.
1、改写
  • The two line graphs illustrate changes in the ownership of three electrical household appliances (washing machines, refrigerators and vacuum cleaners) and the weekly hours spent on housework per household in a single country over the period from 1920 to 2019.
2、Overview
  • Overall, these years witnessed a continuous decline(代替decline trend) in the weekly hours of housework per household, alongside(代替and) a significant rise in the ownership of all three electrical appliances.（一张总体向上趋势，一张总体向下趋势）
3、细节段

  • In 1920, refrigerators were the least common appliance, with less than 5% of households owning one.

    However, their ownership rose sharply over the following decades, surpassing (超过)both washing machines and vacuum cleaners by 1960 and reaching 100% penetration by 1980.

  • By contrast, washing machines and vacuum cleaners were more popular initially, with 40% and 30% of households owning them respectively in 1920.

    Vacuum cleaner ownership saw a steady and continuous increase, hitting 100% in 2000 and remaining at this level until 2019. (维持这个数值直到)

    For washing machines, ownership climbed to 70% in 1960, then dipped slightly to around 63% in 1980, before rising gradually again to just over 70% by the end of the period.（描述先上升，再下降，再上升）

4、细节段

  • Turning to the hours spent on housework, there was a striking downward trend over the whole period.

    In 1920, households spent an average of 50 hours per week on housework, but this figure plummeted to(快速下降) just 20 hours by 1960.

    After this sharp initial decline, the number of hours stayed stable at around 15 hours per week between 1980 and 2000, before falling slightly further to approximately 11 hours in 2019.

3、细节段

  • To begin with, the total number of visitors stood at 1 million in 2010, and rose steadily to approximately 2.7 million by 2015.

    This figure remained stable at 2.7 million in 2016, before surging sharply to a peak of 3.5 million in 2017, the highest level recorded over the period.（最高）

4、细节段

  • In terms of the two visitor types, in 2010, around 0.25 million tourists stayed on cruise ships, while 0.75 million stayed on the island.

    The number of cruise ship visitors fluctuated between 0.25 million and 0.5 million in the first three years, before rising continuously to 2 million by 2017, overtaking the number of island-based visitors in 2016.

    By contrast, the figure for visitors staying on the island saw a steep increase from 0.75 million in 2010 to 1.5 million in 2013, after which it remained relatively stable at around 1.5 million until 2017, with only a minor dip in 2016.（中间下降了一点）
